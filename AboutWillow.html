Willow (1988)

The forgotten lucas film

Willow was born of the creative genius of George Lucas and brought to life by Ron Howard. The movie was a mild success when it was released in 1988 but has grown into a cult classic. So much so, that a sequel series is being developed. As a fan myself, I am excited to talk about Willow and get you caught up on the movie before the series is released on Disney Plus.

Before we get into the fun part where I get to tell you about the best movie ever made. Here is the data that shows how well other Lucas Films did in comparison to Willow. The following list is all Lucas Films movies ranked by their box office performances. So, highest grossing movie at the top and least grossing movie at the bottom. Merchandise

Because of it's relatively weak release, there was not a whole lot of merchandise made to support the movie. There was certainly an initial attempt to help promote the movie before it's releae though. That is evident in the toys and other merchandise that was made at the time. Cast

  • Val Kilmer as Madmartigan

  • Jean Marsh as Queen Bavmorda
  • Patricia Hayes as Fin Raziel

Trivia

Val Kilmer and Joanne Whalley dated and later married after meeting on the set of Willow.


Warwick Davis has played a part in 8 different Star Wars movies or TV series.
